Plastic Use PSA

Plastic Use PSA

Description


For this project, I created a 20-second public service announcement about plastic waste and ocean pollution using Adobe After Effects. I chose a lotion bottle as the central object, connecting it to earlier projects, to represent single-use plastic. The animation follows the bottle as it sinks to the bottom of the ocean, affecting various marine animals along its path.

The aim was to raise awareness of the environmental impact of everyday plastic through a short, visually engaging infomercial-style video. This project allowed me to work with motion design, animation, and storytelling, while keeping a consistent visual theme across multiple pieces.
