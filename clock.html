Natures Clock

Natures Clock

Description


This project is an interactive clock built with P5.js, inspired by a post-apocalyptic world where nature has reclaimed the environment and traditional clocks no longer exist. Instead of showing time in a conventional way, the clock represents a daylight cycle—featuring sunrise, sunset, and nighttime transitions—to reflect the natural passage of time.

Different animals appear throughout the day, such as a bear and bird during daylight hours, and a raccoon at night. These animals emerge based on whether the current hour or minute is odd or even, giving users a subtle and creative way to interpret time. I also added ambient sound effects that change with the time of day, including cicadas in the evening and crickets at night, to enhance the immersive experience.

While the core functionality is in place, this project is still a work in progress. I plan to complete the hand-drawn animations for the animals and replace the temporary Adobe Stock background with an original illustration to fully bring this world to life.
